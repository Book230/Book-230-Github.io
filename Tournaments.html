Tournaments

There are 4 different main tournaments that are played on both tours . They include 250, 500 , 1,000 , and grand slams . 250's are the lowest tier , you earn the least points from it compared to other types of tournaments , the tournament amenities aren't that luxurious , and the prize money is low .
